### Building automation people can trust
TheOneEye is a managed automation company focused on building, operating, and maintaining reliable workflows for modern businesses.

Automation today is powerful, but fragile. Tools execute tasks, yet responsibility often falls through the cracks. When systems break, the cost is usually hidden — lost time, missed opportunities, and growing operational risk.

TheOneEye exists to change that.

We take responsibility for the automations we build, ensuring they continue to work as systems evolve.

---

## What TheOneEye Does

TheOneEye designs and manages custom automation systems that support real business operations.

<div align="center">
  <img src="media/TheOneEyeFirstLook.gif" alt="TheOneEye Platform Overview" width="800">
</div>

We build workflows using a node-based execution platform that can accomplish almost any task. Workflows are composed of configurable nodes that can represent:

* API calls and integrations
* Browser actions and web automation
* Data processing and transformation
* AI and ML inference
* Decision logic and conditional flows
* Validation and quality checks
* Human-in-the-loop steps

The platform is extensible: new node connectors can be developed for any task or system. This allows us to work with nearly any system, including platforms that require login, manual steps, or user interaction.

Our focus is not on building tools, but on delivering outcomes that continue to work over time.

## Our Approach

Automation is not a one-time setup.
It is an ongoing operational responsibility.

Our approach combines:

* Thoughtful workflow design
* Continuous operation and maintenance
* Human ownership and accountability
* Adaptation as tools, websites, and processes change

We don’t expect clients to monitor, debug, or fix automations themselves. The systems we build are actively managed by our team.
